#### 2. Key Drivers of Store Visit Rate: Causal Inference-Based Analysis of Out-of-Home (OOH) Advertising Effectiveness - Mar,2026
To precisely evaluate advertising performance, this study focuses on two core business metrics: Exposure Conversion Rate (measuring the efficiency of generating user interest from ad exposure) and Store Visit Contribution Rate (reflecting the actual impact of ads on offline foot traffic). Leveraging the Double Machine Learning (DML) framework, we aim to isolate the true causal effects of various influencing factors and quantify their relative importance. Ultimately, this research provides actionable budget allocation strategies for advertisers, enhancing the scientific rigor of OOH campaigns and maximizing Return on Investment (ROI).

#### 3. Homogenous Group Analysis - Aug,2025
To precisely quantify the effectiveness of Out-of-Home (OOH) advertising, we employed Propensity Score Matching (PSM) to construct homogeneous control groups. Following rigorous homogeneity tests to ensure group comparability, we evaluated the advertising exposure impact by comparing key performance indicators (KPIs) between the two groups.
